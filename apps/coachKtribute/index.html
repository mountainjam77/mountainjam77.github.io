Mike Krzyzewski

"Coach K"

Coach K is the first men's coach to win 1,000 NCAA Division I basketball games.

Here is a timeline of Coach K's life and achievements:

  • 1947 - Born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1969 - Krzyzewski served in the United States Army and directed service teams for three years.
  • 1974 - Started his coaching career as an assistant on Knight's staff with the Indiana Hoosiers during their historic 1974–75 season.
  • 1975 - After one year with Indiana, Krzyzewski returned to West Point as head coach of the Army Cadets. He led the Cadets to a 73–59 record and one NIT berth in five seasons.
  • 1980 - Krzyzewski was named the head coach at Duke University. After a few rebuilding seasons, he and the Blue Devils became a fixture on the national basketball scene with 31 NCAA Tournament berths in the past 32 years and 22 consecutive from 1996 to 2017. His Duke teams have won 13 ACC Championships, been to 12 Final Fours, and won five NCAA tournament National Championships.
  • 1984 - He was an assistant coach to the USA team which won the gold medal at the Olympics.
  • 1986 - He was National Coach of the Year.
  • 1989 - He was the Naismith College Coach of the Year.
  • 1991 - He won his first National Championship.
  • 1992 - He won his second National Championship pulling off back to back Championships, which hadn't been done since UCLA in '72 and '73. He was also the assistant coach to the USA team which won the gold medal at the Olympics.
  • 2001 - He won his third national championship and was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame.
  • 2008 - He won a gold medal at the Beijing Olympics as head coach of Team USA.
  • 2010 - Krzyzewski won his fourth national championship. He also coached in his 1,000th game as the Duke head coach.
  • 2012 - He won his second gold medal at the London Olympics as head coach of Team USA.
  • 2015 - Krzyzewski won his 1,000th game, when Duke defeated St. John's in Madison Square Garden. He is the first men's coach to win 1,000 NCAA Division I basketball games. Krzyzewski also won his 5th NCAA championship, when Duke defeated Wisconsin in the title game.
  • 2016 - Winning against Yale in the 2016 NCAA tournament on March 19, Krzyzewski became the all-time winningest coach in the NCAA Division I tournament with 90 total wins. He won his third gold medal at the Rio de Janeiro Olympics as head coach of Team USA.
